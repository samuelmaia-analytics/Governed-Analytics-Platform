# 003 - Why Explainable Risk Scoring

## Context
- Privacy publication decisions must be defensible for governance and compliance discussions.
- A single opaque score is insufficient for remediation planning.

## Decision
- Keep privacy risk scoring explainable with component points, rationale, recommendations, and publication recommendation.

## Consequences
- Clearer executive storytelling and auditability.
- Easier prioritization of masking/anonymization/removal actions.
- Slightly more output complexity and testing effort.

## Alternatives considered
- Black-box aggregate score only.
- Fully manual qualitative assessment.
